❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I join the club?

Fill out our registration form on the Join The Club page or email us directly. We'll be in touch within 48 hours.

Is there an age limit?

We currently play adult cricket (18+) but welcome players of all ages within that bracket. We're also exploring junior development programmes.

Do I need experience?

Not at all! We welcome complete beginners through to experienced players. We have coaches who can help you develop your skills.

Where do you train?

We train and play home matches at Victoria Park, Belfast BT4 1LS. Training times are confirmed after registration.

How much does it cost?

Membership fees help cover league registration, ground hire, and equipment. Contact us for the latest membership pricing for the 2026 season.

How can I sponsor the club?

Visit our Sponsors page to learn about partnership opportunities or contact us directly for a sponsorship pack.
